Process

  • Create Author Account on Envato Elements
  • We need to upload high-quality digital products such as design templates, graphics, stock photos, fonts, music tracks, etc.
  • Our Assets should meet the specific requirements of the Envato marketplace (e.g., file format, resolution, and description).
  • After creating the product, we need to submit it to the relevant Envato marketplace for review.
  • We'll need to include:
    • - Description: A clear and concise description of your product.
    • - Preview Images/Video: High-quality visuals that showcase your product.
    • - Tags & Categories: Choose relevant tags to help buyers find your item.
  • Envato's reviewers will evaluate the submission based on quality, originality, and marketability. If the submission passes, it will be listed in the marketplace.
  • If the submission doesn't meet the quality standards, we’ll receive feedback on what to improve before resubmitting.
  • We can set our own prices for items (within some guidelines) or rely on Envato’s pricing suggestions based on similar products.
  • Envato takes a percentage of each sale. The exact commission depends on author level (based on the number of sales you've made).
  • If we choose to sell exclusively on Envato, we can earn up to 87% of the sale price, but Envato takes a cut (ranging from 12.5% to 37.5%).
  • If we sell on multiple platforms, we earn less, typically around 55% of the sale price.
  • Earnings Breakdown:
    • - Item Price: $10
    • - Envato Fee (Exclusive): $1.50–$3.75 (varies by sales volume)
    • - Author Earnings: Around $6–$8 per sale for an exclusive author.
  • Payment Methods: Envato pays through PayPal, Payoneer, and bank transfers, with a minimum payout threshold (usually around $50).

Process

Professional Website: Your website is the foundation of your SEO brand. It should be easy to navigate, mobile-friendly, and optimized for search engines (yes, your own SEO matters!). Showcase your services clearly and include case studies, client testimonials, and results to build trust.

  • SEO Services Page:

    Highlight the range of SEO services you offer (e.g., on-page SEO, technical SEO, content marketing, link building, local SEO, etc.).

  • Case Studies:

    Provide real-world examples of your work. Showcase how you've helped businesses increase their traffic, rankings, or sales.

  • Blog:

    Write regularly about SEO best practices, industry trends, and case studies to demonstrate your expertise and attract organic traffic.

  • Landing Pages:

    Create specific landing pages for each SEO service you offer. Tailor these pages with targeted keywords for specific client needs (e.g., "local SEO for small businesses" or "eCommerce SEO").

  • Clear Call to Action (CTA): Every page should have a clear CTA—whether it’s for a consultation, free audit, or contact form. Make it easy for potential clients to take the next step.

Upselling Opportunities: You can upsell related services. For example, a client who buys a logo design might also be interested in getting a full website built, or someone buying an eBook may need a promotional website.

Different Market Segments: These services appeal to different client needs—startups, individuals, professionals, and even small businesses—which expands your target market.
